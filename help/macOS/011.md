## *How to set up **Unlock with Apple Watch?***

<br />

Before you can use your Apple Watch to unlock Authenticator, youâ€™ll need to set up [**Auto Unlock on your Mac**](https://support.apple.com/en-gb/HT206995). Then follow these steps:

*1. Open and unlock **Authenticator**.*
*2. Press on **Settings** in the top left corner, and find  **Unlock with Apple Watch**.*
